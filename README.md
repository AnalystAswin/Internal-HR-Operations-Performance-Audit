# Internal-HR-Operations-Performance-Audit
This is an Internal HR &amp; Operations Performance Audit. It showcases foundational to intermediate data manipulation. It tracks employee demographics, salaries, job titles, department allocations, and project timelines.
# 🏢 Corporate HR & Operational Performance Audit (SQL)

## 📌 Business Problem
As companies scale, human resource data often becomes fragmented across isolated tables (e.g., employee details, department budgets, and active project timelines). Without a unified querying framework, HR leadership struggles to identify salary inequities, track department-level resource allocation, audit project deadlines, or isolate high-performing teams.

This project utilizes relational database queries to audit corporate resource distribution, evaluate structural department overhead, and surface immediate operational bottlenecks in project delivery timelines.

## 🛠️ Tools & Database Techniques Used
* **SQL (Relational Querying Engine):** Engineered for relational data extraction.
* **Multi-Table Aggregations:** Joining disparate dimensions (`INNER JOIN`, `LEFT JOIN`) across employee profiles, payroll brackets, and operational assignments.
* **Conditional Data Filtering:** Leveraging `GROUP BY` and `HAVING` clauses to isolate structural anomalies and performance deficits.
* **Pattern Analysis:** Deploying text wildcards (`LIKE`) and conditional constraints to segment human capital data.

## 🧼 Database Architecture & Query Structure
The analytical scripts are organized to solve escalating corporate operations problems:
1. **Demographic & Payroll Profiling:** Isolating base employee records, identifying top-tier salary brackets, and evaluating department-level headcounts.
2. **Departmental Financial Auditing:** Summarizing total capital expenditures on payroll by department, tracking average compensation footprints, and flagging structural budget impacts.
3. **Operational Project Tracking:** Cross-referencing employee project assignments against active deadlines to audit resource bandwidth and delivery delays.

## 📊 Core Business Insights Extracted
* **Resource Discrepancies:** Identified exact departments experiencing critical resource shortages or understaffing relative to their active project loads.
* **Compensation Benchmarking:** Visualized historical payroll distributions to help management audit pay equity across identical job functions.
* **Project Delivery Risk:** Highlighted high-risk projects lagging due to inadequate staff allocation or structural dependencies.

## 💡 Strategic Business Impact
* **Data-Backed Workforce Planning:** Replaced manual Excel headcount tracking with an automated SQL auditing framework, enabling HR to spot cross-departmental imbalances instantly.
* **Optimized Resource Allocation:** Provided project management offices (PMO) with immediate visibility into employee bandwidth, reducing project bottlenecks by aligning active headcounts with high-priority timelines.
